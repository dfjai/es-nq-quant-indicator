# Signal Definitions

## Sig
Current signal state such as Scanning, BUY EARLY, BUY A+, SELL EARLY, or SELL A+.

## Conf
Internal confidence score derived from signal alignment and confirmation strength.

## Vol
Volume state, usually Bull Vol, Bear Vol, or Normal Vol.

## Z
Relative position or stretch from the model mean / structure. Useful for identifying fair value versus extension.

## Daily
Higher-timeframe directional context.

## Loc
Price location relative to value, VWAP, or structural zone.

## Sigs
Number of aligned conditions currently supporting the signal.

## WR
Placeholder for future win-rate tracking or signal performance tracking.
